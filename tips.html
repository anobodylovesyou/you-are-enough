Quick Tips to Fight Depression

Get Moving

Exercise, even a short walk, can help release endorphins and improve your mood.

Talk to Someone

Reach out to a friend, family member, or professional. Talking about your feelings is healing.

Eat Well

A healthy diet can support your mental health. Try eating more whole foods, like fruits and veggies.

Practice Mindfulness

Meditation and deep breathing exercises can help calm your mind and reduce stress.
